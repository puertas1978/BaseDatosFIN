1.1.Regional Public Entities

The Spanish Constitution, in its article 148/13, indicates that it is the competence of the each region to "promote economic development within the objectives set by national economic policy".  Therefore, they are public entities:

State-level entities

Those that each community has established as bodies limited to the autonomous area and their own actions.

In both cases, it is a question of providing information for the promotion of import and export business activities, being alternative and complementary access to both options.  Public entities in the regional field, including the information service on the financing of foreign trade, are explained in the following sections.

EXTENDA (ANDALUCIA TRADE)

The autonomous community of Andalusia manages all those issues related to the internationalization of Andalusian companies through the Andalusian Agency of Foreign Promotion (Extenda). This regional body offers extensive information, consulting, and promotion and training services for companies in the internationalization phase.

As regards the financing of the exporter, Andalusia has a budget specifically earmarked for this purpose (Fund for the Internationalization of the Andalusian Economy), managed by Extenda, which is intended annually for the granting of loans and guarantees. These loans are granted on market terms to the applicant companies in accordance with the established conditions. In no case can be considered subsidies or public aid.

Canal Youtube Andalucia TRADE. Presentation of Andalucia Trade

Regional public entities with competence in promoting the development of the economy are the first option of consultation for small and medium-sized enterprises wishing to initiate an internationalization process. The proximity of the institutions and, therefore, their deep knowledge of the characteristics of the industrial and commercial fabric of the region, constitute a strength to the system that companies should not miss.
