2.4. Challenge 2: Database

Goals

Build a relational database of organizations that provide financial support, assistance, or grants for export and international sales

The objective of this exercise is for the student to learn how to create a relational database containing information about state and regional organizations that support companies' international activities. Additionally, they should utilize the filtering function to organize and search for information within the database.

Development

Task 1: Complete the activities provided in the exelearning material to familiarize yourself with relational databases.

Task 2: Creation of a Relational Database for Organizations Supporting International Business Activities

The entities in the database will be as follows:

PRIVATE FINANCE/INSTITUTIONS.
Find 3 trade-related financial services offered by 3 banks of your choice. Provide the following information: Bank, financial service offered, and conditions required for application.

PUBLIC FINANCE/INSTITUTIONS.
You need to gather information from the following organizations:

Chamber of Commerce: Find 5 grants.
Extenda: Find 2 commercial missions (inbound and outbound) and 2 trade fairs where a company can promote itself internationally, specifying the conditions.
ICEX: ICEX Next and ICEX Enisa conditions.
COFIDES: Find assistance services and conditions for at least 5 eligible actions.
CESCE: Find at least 3 state insurance services covered by this entity and include conditions.
You must create the database to perform any related queries. (Use Dbase o Access) and upload to portfolio
