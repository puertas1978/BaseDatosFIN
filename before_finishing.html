My personal reflection, What have I learn?

In these challenges, we have worked on different concepts and tools that will be very useful for identifying funding opportunities for our companies and clients.

Before we finish, we have to consider three questions that we should answer in our learning journal:

  • Can these challenges be connected to something I already knew?
  • Have I learned something that contradicts previous ideas I had about international finance?
  • What opportunities does what I have learned open up for me in the future?
