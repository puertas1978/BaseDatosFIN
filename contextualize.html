FINANCIACIÓN INTERNACIONAL

This Open Educational Resource (REA) aims to serve as classroom material for the Bilingual International Financing module, part of the Bilingual International Trade Training. In this unit, we will focus on various organizations that provide support and international financing to businesses and create relational databases accordingly.

How is the course structured?

The resource consists of 3 Challenges. Each challenge presents a series of tasks related to each content block of the module. Additionally, we will have training pills that will introduce and present, from a theoretical standpoint, each challenge. Here you can find a summary of the work to be done

How we are going to work

We will begin by working on and getting to know the organizations that support the internationalization of businesses and understanding the information they publish on their respective websites. Next, we will identify the necessary information to create a database and learn how relational databases are structured. In pairs, we will create two relational databases: one for institutions and assistance for internationalization, and another for clients. We will also learn how to update and maintain them.

Work management

Each team must have two tools that will be fundamental for their performance:

A Paddlet board, where we will have the list of tools, tutorials, videos, or resources that you will need to solve the challenges.
A project portfolio that will serve to compile the work done in the challenge and as an instrument for its evaluation. You must use Symbaloo.

Portfolio

The portfolio is the document that will collect the work that, as a project team, we will be developing in each challenge. It can be created on Drive using the template we attach or on paper as a project folder where we will keep evidence of the work.

Structure

Title. We will put the title of the challenge we are undertaking, as well as the corresponding category it belongs to.

Content. Once the challenge is completed, we will include the following sections in the portfolio:

1. Evidence of the work carried out in the challenge: the start and end date of the challenge, as well as the development of its activities. Also, the resources we have created and a sample of them (for example, photos of mock-ups, resources, etc.).
2. Reflection on teamwork.

Evaluation

We use this rúbrica de evaluación del porfolio to assess the quality.
